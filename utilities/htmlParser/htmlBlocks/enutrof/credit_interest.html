Credit Interest

This passive lends Enutrofs the means to pay back their creditors by making their damage more efficient. When an indebted target dies, a Silver Mine appears under the target and under its killer. Plus, Broke drops two purses instead of one.

Normal effects

When a Indebted target gets KO'd.
- Places a Silver Mine under the target and its killer
- (If there is no other within 2 cells)
Broke drops two purses instead of one
10% Damage inflicted
