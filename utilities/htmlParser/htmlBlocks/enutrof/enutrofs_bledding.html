Enutrof's Blessing

The god Enutrof is generous… sometimes. This passive reduces the Seismic Wealth recast time so Enutrofs can consistently take advantage of their bonuses. Plus, it regenerates their health points each time they use a WP spell.

Normal effects

When the Enutrof consumes 1 WP:
- Heals: 8% of the target's missing HP
Seismic Wealth:
- Reduces the recast time by 1 turn
