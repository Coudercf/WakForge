Faking It

Enutrofs make the most of their advanced age and feign death by heart attack... Remember, you can trick a thousand Iops once, but tricking the same Iop a thousand times is a little more difficult! In addition, this passive offers Enutrofs a great amount of Armor when they transform into Drhellzerkers.

Normal effects

If the Enutrof is KO'd and on a :
- Faking It (Lvl. 1)
Modifies Drhellzerker:
- Armor: 20% of target's max HP when transforming
